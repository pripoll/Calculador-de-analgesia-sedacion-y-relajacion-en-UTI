Information

This tool is for educational use. It does not provide information for initial bolus doses. Calculations for patients with BMI > 30 are automatically adjusted to Ideal Body Weight (IBW) for hydrophilic drugs and Adjusted Body Weight (ABW) for lipophilic drugs.

About

ASR Infusion Precision Navigator. A web application designed to assist healthcare professionals in calculating correct infusion rates for analgesia, sedation, and neuromuscular blockade.
